What can you do with Ripple?

1. Send links to your friends Found an interesting link you want to share with a few people? Paste or search for it and send it with a couple of taps.
2. Chat about specific links Got an opinion about a link you're sent? Ripple is perfect for keeping the conversation going.
3. Forward links you receive Want to pass on an interesting link to other people? Forwarding it with your own message attached couldn't be easier.

Enjoy!
